## Equation 6 as a loss function

The original equation:

$$
\rho\left(\frac{\partial {v}}{\partial t}+{v} \cdot \nabla {v}\right)=\frac{1}{c} {J} \times {B}-\nabla P+\rho {g}+\nabla \cdot(\nu \rho \nabla {v})
$$

For $$v_r$$ only:

$$
\rho\left(\frac{\partial {v}}{\partial t}+{v} \cdot \nabla {v}\right)=\frac{1}{c} {J} \times {B}-\nabla P+\rho {g}+\nabla \cdot(\nu \rho \nabla {v})
$$

Static approximation:

$$
\rho\left(-\Omega_{\text{rot}}\frac{\partial{v_r}}{\partial \phi}+{v} \cdot \nabla {v}\right)=\frac{1}{c} {J} \times {B}-\nabla P+\rho {g}+\nabla \cdot(\nu \rho \nabla {v})
$$
